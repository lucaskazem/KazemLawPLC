Jonathan B. Kazem

Jonathan B. Kazem is a founding Partner in the firm of Kazem Law, PLC, where he dedicates the majority of his practice to fighting for those who have been injured by the negligence of others. Mr. Kazem has been practicing personal injury law for more than twenty years. Mr. Kazem received his Juris Doctor from Stetson University College of Law in 1999. He is admitted to the Virginia Bar, Florida Bar, District of Colombia Bar, Pennsylvania Bar, and The United States District Court for the Eastern District of Virginia Bar.
