About Us

At Kazem Law, we are committed to providing exceptional legal representation and personalized service to our clients. Led by our esteemed attorney, Jonathan Kazem, who brings over 20 years of experience to the practice of law, we have established a reputation for successfully handling a diverse range of legal matters.

Our firm specializes in two primary areas of practice: personal injury claims and contract disputes. We understand the significant impact these legal issues can have on your life, finances, and well-being. Whether you've suffered an injury due to someone else's negligence or are involved in a contract dispute, our team is prepared to fight vigorously on your behalf.

Jonathan Kazem is a seasoned legal professional with an impressive track record. He earned his Juris Doctor from Stetson University College of Law in 1999, and since then, he has been admitted to practice law in various jurisdictions, including the Virginia Bar, Florida Bar, District of Columbia Bar, Pennsylvania Bar, and The United States District Court for the Eastern District of Virginia Bar.

If you have questions about how the Firm can help you with your claim or defense, contact an attorney at anytime. Kazem Law will be happy to answer your questions and schedule an appointment to meet with you.

Our Practice Areas

This firm represents clients with a broad area of legal concerns. We have expertise in criminal and civil litigation and commercial law.

Possession of Controlled Substances

Our office is well-versed in handling cases related to the possession of controlled substances. We understand that facing charges involving controlled substances can be overwhelming and may have serious legal consequences. That's why we are here to offer you the guidance, support, and zealous representation you need during this challenging time.

Driving While Under the Influence

We understand that facing charges of Driving While Under the Influence (DWI/DUI) can be a distressing and life-altering experience. If you or a loved one is dealing with a DWI/DUI charge, our team of experienced attorneys is here to provide you with steadfast legal representation and guidance.

Reckless Driving

At Kazem Law, we are here to protect your rights and defend you against charges of reckless driving. If you find yourself facing reckless driving allegations, it's essential to seek immediate legal representation. Our office is highly experienced in handling reckless driving cases and will work diligently to achieve the best possible outcome for your situation.

Assault and Battery

We are dedicated to providing aggressive and skillful defense representation for clients facing charges of assault and battery. If you or a loved one is confronting these serious allegations, it is crucial to have a strong legal defense to protect your rights and future.
